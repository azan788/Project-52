Penguin Facts

Cicada Facts

  1. Using power tools near a cicada might make it think you are another cicada and land on you
  2. Only male cicadas make that loud sound you hear
  3. 7 species are called periodical cicadas because the only come out every 17 or 13 years

Camel Facts

  1. A one humped camel is called a dromedary camel, an two humped one is Bactrian
  2. Camels can completely shut their noses in case of sandstorms
  3. Camels can travel at speeds up to 40 miles per hour, or 64 kilometers per hour
